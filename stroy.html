Espresso Essence – Our Story

Nestled in the heart of the city, Espresso Essence was born from a simple belief: coffee is not just a beverage — it's an experience. A moment to pause, to connect, to reflect. Our founders, passionate coffee lovers and seekers of simple joys, dreamt of a space where time slows down and every sip tells a story.

The name Espresso Essence speaks to our mission — to capture the very essence of espresso: bold, rich, and authentic. From sourcing the finest beans to perfecting the crema on every shot, our craft is fueled by passion and precision.

Step inside and you’ll find more than coffee. You’ll find handpicked vintage décor, the aroma of freshly ground beans, handwritten menus, and the gentle hum of conversations. Whether you’re starting your morning, writing your next novel, or simply taking a break — you’re always welcome here.

At Espresso Essence, we serve more than just drinks.
We serve warmth, community, and a daily dose of inspiration.
